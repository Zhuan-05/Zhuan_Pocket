When start the wheat cleaning machine progress need make sure Check that no wheat is stuck in the gap. if it gets wet, wheal will be harden and could cause a blockage especially the first bin (301 to 305).
  
A blockage in the tempering line will cause wheat to back fill into bucket elevator  
Once the elevator boot is fully choked, the only remedy is to open the inspection door and bag out at least eight bags of wheat by hand.  
If the blockage is not cleared in time, the belt may stall and ultimately break, resulting in extend downtime and need repair.
  
Before transferring to the pre-tempering bins (306, 307, 317, and 318), check that the bins are empty. Then wait approximately 10 minutes before starting, as the machine needs time to prepare for operation.
  
roller mills check
1. Set identical grinding force on both rolls to obtain the same break
1. Collect a small sample of break flour from each side and spread it evenly on the open palms. Visually compare brightness and particle size
1. any discrepancy indicates unequal grinding pressure and requires immediate roll adjustment.
1. If the product pipe on top of any stand overflows, identify the restriction : inspect downstream for blockage or increase the roll speed of that mill
